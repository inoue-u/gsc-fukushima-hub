Fukushima Study TourFukushima Study Tour

Fukushima Study Tourは，浜通りを中心に地域の現場と向き合い，対話と体験を通じて学びを深めるプログラムである．

  • 目的：学びの場の創出，関係人口の形成，継続的な協働の起点づくり
  • 形式：講義・現地訪問・対話セッション

参加案内は公式Instagramで告知する．

The Fukushima Study Tour is an immersive program that engages with local realities—especially in Hamadori—through visits, conversations, and learning sessions.

  • Goals: learning, building lasting relationships, and creating starting points for collaboration
  • Format: briefings, site visits, and dialogue sessions

Participation updates are announced via our official Instagram.
