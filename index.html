About

Global Shapers Community Fukushima Hub is part of the World Economic Forum Global Shapers Community. The hub connects young leaders, entrepreneurs, researchers, and innovators to promote regional innovation and social impact in Fukushima.

Projects

Fukushima Study Tour connects global young leaders with entrepreneurs and innovators across Fukushima. Participants explore regional revitalization, technology innovation, and social entrepreneurship.

FAQ

What is Global Shapers Community?
Global Shapers Community is an initiative of the World Economic Forum connecting young leaders around the world.

How can I join Fukushima Hub?
Recruitment is conducted annually. Announcements are made through Instagram and the WEF website.
